About Us

We are a non-profit group organizing regular blood donation camps across India. Our mission is to encourage voluntary blood donation and ensure that no one dies due to a shortage of blood.

Our Vision

To build a society where donating blood becomes a regular,selfless habit that saves countless lives.

Facts About Blood Donation

  • 1 unit of blood can save up to 3 lives.
  • It only takes 15 minutes to donate.
  • Healthy adults can donate every 3 months.
